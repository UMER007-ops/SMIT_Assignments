Pakistani Dishes and Ingredients

  • Biryani
    1. Rice — 500g
    2. Chicken — 1 kg
    3. Yogurt — 1 cup
    4. Onions — 3 large, sliced
    5. Tomatoes — 2 medium
    6. Spices:
      1. Red chili powder — 2 tsp
      2. Turmeric — ½ tsp
      3. Biryani masala — 2 tbsp
      4. Salt — to taste
    7. Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Chicken Karahi
    1. Chicken — 1 kg (cut into pieces)
    2. Tomatoes — 4 to 5 medium, chopped
    3. Green chilies — 4 to 5
    4. Ginger garlic paste — 2 tbsp
    5. Oil — ½ cup
    6. Spices:
      1. Red chili powder — 1 tsp
      2. Salt — to taste
      3. Black pepper — ½ tsp
    7. Fresh coriander — for garnish
  • Haleem
    1. Wheat grains — 1 cup
    2. Lentils (masoor, moong, chana) — 1 cup mixed
    3. Beef or chicken — 500g
    4. Onions — 2 medium, fried
    5. Ginger garlic paste — 2 tbsp
    6. Spices:
      1. Haleem masala — 2 tbsp
      2. Salt — to taste
      3. Red chili powder — 1 tsp
    7. Garnish:
      1. Fresh coriander
      2. Lemon slices
      3. Fried onions
  • Chapli Kebab
    1. Minced beef — 500g
    2. Onion — 1, finely chopped
    3. Tomato — 1, finely chopped
    4. Green chilies — 2, chopped
    5. Gram flour — 2 tbsp
    6. Spices:
      1. Cumin seeds — 1 tsp
      2. Coriander seeds — 1 tsp (crushed)
      3. Red chili flakes — 1 tsp
      4. Salt — to taste
  • Nihari
    1. Beef shank — 1 kg
    2. Onions — 2 large, sliced
    3. Wheat flour — 2 tbsp (for thickening)
    4. Ginger garlic paste — 2 tbsp
    5. Oil — ½ cup
    6. Spices:
      1. Nihari masala — 3 tbsp
      2. Red chili powder — 1 tsp
      3. Salt — to taste
    7. Garnish:
      1. Fresh ginger — thinly sliced
      2. Lemon wedges
      3. Fresh coriander
